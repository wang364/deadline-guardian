# Privacy Policy

## 1. Introduction

Thank you for using the Jira Issue Reminder application. This Privacy Policy is designed to help you understand how we collect, use, store, and protect your information, as well as the rights you are entitled to. By using this application, you agree to our collection and use of your information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

## 2. Information Collection

The Jira Issue Reminder application primarily operates within the Atlassian Jira environment to help users set up and manage JQL queries for issue reminders. The information we collect and process includes but is not limited to:

- **JQL Query Content**: The JQL query statements you configure in the application
- **Application Usage Data**: Such as feature usage, error logs, etc.
- **User Identifiers**: Necessary information for authentication and authorization

## 3. Information Use

The information we collect will be used for the following purposes:

- Providing, maintaining, and improving application functionality
- Processing JQL queries and generating corresponding reminders
- Ensuring application security and stability
- Responding to user support requests and feedback

## 4. Information Storage

- All data is stored in secure storage provided by the Atlassian Forge platform
- Data is retained only for the period necessary to achieve the purposes described in this Privacy Policy
- We employ industry-standard security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction

## 5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data to any third parties. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

- With your explicit consent
- To comply with applicable laws, regulations, or legal procedures
- To protect the rights, property, or safety of the Jira Issue Reminder application, its users, or the public

## 6. Data Controller

The data controller for this application is the application developer. To contact us, please reach out through the application support channels.

## 7. User Rights

Under applicable data protection laws, you may have the following rights:

- Access your personal data
- Correct inaccurate personal data
- Delete your personal data (under specific conditions)
- Restrict processing of your personal data
- Data portability rights
- Object to processing of your personal data

## 8. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make significant changes, we will notify you through in-app notifications or other appropriate means. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.

## 9. Governing Law
